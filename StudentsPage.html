Welcome to the Students section. Here you will be able to:

1) Add Students to your Private school.

2) Check the Students who already are studying in your private school.

3) Enroll Students to Courses. Click on Enroll Students To Courses. A new window will appear. Click on the Course Form you want to enroll the Student. Drag the Student to the Course you clicked previously. To delete a Student. Drag the Student from the Course List to the Student List.

4) Check each Student based on the Course he is studying. Click on the course and check the students that are enrolled in it.

5) Check the students that belong to more than one Courses. Click on the students that you will see and check the courses they are enrolled in.
