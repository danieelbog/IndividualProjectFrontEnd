Welcome to the Trainers section. Here you will be able to:

1) Add Trainers to your Private school.

2) Check how many trainers are already working in your private school.

3) Enroll Trainers to Courses. Click on Enroll Trainers To Courses. A new window will appear. Click on the Course Form you want to enroll the Trainer. Drag the Trainer to the Course you clicked previously. To delete a Trainer. Drag the Trainer from the Course List to the Trainer List.

4) Check each Trainer based on the Course he is teaching. Click on the Course and check its respective Trainers.

5) Put Marks for Assginments for each Student based on Trainers feedback.
